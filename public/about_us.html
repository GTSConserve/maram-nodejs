Why Medagg?
Types of Patient/Medagg Users

Medagg prioritizes your medical needs by providing you with direct appointments to the best physicians. We enable you to extract the most out of your medical treatment by upfront providing you with the essential costs and price comparisons to alternative consultations.

Our Care Custodians will walk you through all of the above steps and help you make the right healthcare decisions.

We help the 3 types of patients

Patients who know that a certain surgery or procedure is their solution and are looking for hospital options where this surgery/procedure can be undertaken.

Patients who have an illness or a symptom but don’t know where to start.

Patients who have been advised of a certain treatment but are looking for a second opinion.

What is that Medagg does differently?

We are not a tele-consultation or an appointment booking platform. We are a team of professionals who take your entire health incident under our custody and handle EVERYTHING!

The common man as a patient does not know the various options available in front of him to avail of the most economical but still clinically superior treatment. We bring about this awareness by exploring and comparing the available options for our patients. We execute the entire treatment with one of us always by your side physically!
